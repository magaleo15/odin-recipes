Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of fresh milk cream
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 smashed garlic cloves
  • 450g of fettucini dry pasta
  • 110g of fine grinded parmesan cheese
  • fresh grinded black pepper

How to prepare

  1. Wait it to cool for a bit and add the garlic, the pepper and the dry herbs. Mix it up well.
  2. In a big frying pan put the butter, after it melts, add the garlic and let cook until you start feeling the smell, for like 20s
  3. Add the recuded cream and let it boil in low heat
  4. Cook your fettucini aldente drain it and reserve 1 cup of the cooking water
  5. Add the pasta to the frying pan and with the cream and mix it up with the parmesan and a good amount of black pepper (I love it!).
  6. Add the cooking water, enough to get to a creamy texture.
  7. Serve it immediately
